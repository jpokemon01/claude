# How to Forward an Email — Android Phone

This guide is for forwarding an email on an **Android phone**.
We will forward the email to: **takeo_inoue_re@yahoo.com**

---

## What is "Forwarding"?

Forwarding means:
**You received an email → You send that same email to Takeo**

That is it. Very simple.

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### STEP 1 — Find the Gmail App

Look at your Android phone screen.

Find the app that looks like a colorful letter **"M"** (red, blue, yellow, green).

That is the **Gmail** app.

**Tap it** to open.

---

### STEP 2 — Find the Email You Want to Forward

After Gmail opens, you will see a list of emails.

**Tap the email** that you want to forward to Takeo.

---

### STEP 3 — Scroll Down to the Bottom

After you open the email, **scroll down with your finger** to the very bottom of the email.

At the bottom you will see two buttons:

- **← Reply** (sends back to the person who sent you the email — do NOT tap this)
- **→ Forward** (sends to a new person — tap this one)

**Tap "Forward"**

---

### STEP 4 — Type the Email Address

A new screen will open. It looks like a blank email.

At the top you will see a box that says **"To"**.

**Tap that box** and carefully type:

**takeo_inoue_re@yahoo.com**

Go slowly. Check each letter:
- t - a - k - e - o - _ - i - n - o - u - e - _ - r - e - @ - y - a - h - o - o - . - c - o - m

There are **two underscores** ( _ ):
- One between "takeo" and "inoue"
- One between "inoue" and "re"

---

### STEP 5 — Press Send

After you finish typing the email address, look at the **top right corner** of the screen.

You will see a **paper airplane icon** (✈).

**Tap the paper airplane** to send.

---

### STEP 6 — You Are Done!

The email has been forwarded to Takeo.

You do not need to do anything else.

---

## How to Check It Was Sent

If you want to make sure it was sent:

1. Tap the **three lines** (☰) in the top left corner of Gmail
2. Scroll down and tap **"Sent"**
3. You will see the email you just forwarded

If you see it there — it was sent successfully!

---

## Remember

| Do This | Not This |
|---------|----------|
| Tap **Forward** | Do not tap Reply |
| Type **takeo_inoue_re@yahoo.com** | Check spelling carefully |
| Tap the **paper airplane ✈** to send | Do not close the app before sending |

---

## If You Cannot Find the "Forward" Button

Sometimes the Forward button is hidden. Try this:

1. Open the email
2. Scroll all the way to the bottom
3. Look for **three dots** (⋮) in the bottom right corner
4. Tap the three dots
5. You will see a menu — tap **"Forward"**

---

That's all! You did it.
