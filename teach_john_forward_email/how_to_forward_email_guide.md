# How to Forward an Email — Beginner's Guide

This guide will teach you how to forward an email to someone.
**Forwarding** means sending an email you received to another person.

In this guide, we will forward emails to: **takeo_inoue_re@yahoo.com**

---

## What Does "Forward" Mean?

When someone sends you an email, you can **share that email with another person**.
This is called **forwarding**.

Example:
- Your friend sends you an email with important information
- You want Takeo to also see that email
- You **forward** it to takeo_inoue_re@yahoo.com

---

## PART 1 — Forward Email on iPhone (Mail App)

### Step 1 — Open the Mail App
- Look for the **Mail** app on your iPhone (it looks like a white envelope on a blue background)
- Tap it to open

### Step 2 — Find the Email You Want to Forward
- You will see a list of emails
- Tap the email you want to forward

### Step 3 — Open the Forward Option
- After opening the email, look at the **bottom of the screen**
- You will see a **curved arrow pointing right** (↩ or ↪)
- Tap that arrow
- A menu will pop up — tap **"Forward"**

### Step 4 — Enter the Email Address
- A new screen will open that looks like a blank email
- At the top you will see a box that says **"To:"**
- Tap that box and type:
  **takeo_inoue_re@yahoo.com**
- Double-check that you typed it correctly — one wrong letter means the email will not arrive

### Step 5 — Send the Email
- After typing the address, tap the **blue arrow button** (looks like ▶) in the top right corner
- The email is now sent!

---

## PART 2 — Forward Email on Android Phone (Gmail App)

### Step 1 — Open the Gmail App
- Look for the **Gmail** app on your Android phone (it looks like a colorful "M" envelope)
- Tap it to open

### Step 2 — Find the Email You Want to Forward
- You will see a list of emails (called your **Inbox**)
- Tap the email you want to forward

### Step 3 — Open the Forward Option
- Scroll down to the **bottom of the email**
- You will see a button that says **"Forward"** (with an arrow pointing right →)
- Tap **"Forward"**

### Step 4 — Enter the Email Address
- A new screen will open
- Tap the box at the top that says **"To"**
- Type:
  **takeo_inoue_re@yahoo.com**
- Make sure the spelling is exactly correct

### Step 5 — Send the Email
- Tap the **paper airplane button** (✈) in the top right corner
- The email is now sent!

---

## PART 3 — Forward Email on a Computer (Gmail in Web Browser)

### Step 1 — Open Gmail
- Open your internet browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, etc.)
- Go to: **gmail.com**
- Sign in with your email and password if asked

### Step 2 — Find the Email You Want to Forward
- On the left side you will see **"Inbox"**
- Click on the email you want to forward

### Step 3 — Open the Forward Option
- Scroll down to the **bottom of the email**
- You will see a button that says **"Forward"** (with an arrow →)
- Click **"Forward"**

### Step 4 — Enter the Email Address
- A small window will appear at the bottom of your screen
- Click inside the **"To"** box
- Type:
  **takeo_inoue_re@yahoo.com**

### Step 5 — Send the Email
- Click the blue **"Send"** button
- Done! The email has been forwarded.

---

## PART 4 — Forward Email on a Computer (Outlook)

### Step 1 — Open Outlook
- Open Outlook on your computer
- Or go to: **outlook.com** in your browser

### Step 2 — Find the Email You Want to Forward
- Click on the email you want to forward in your Inbox

### Step 3 — Click Forward
- At the top of the email, click the button that says **"Forward"**
- It has an arrow pointing right →

### Step 4 — Enter the Email Address
- Click inside the **"To"** box
- Type:
  **takeo_inoue_re@yahoo.com**

### Step 5 — Send the Email
- Click the **"Send"** button
- Done!

## Important Tips

| Tip | Why It Matters |
|-----|----------------|
| Check the email address spelling carefully | One wrong letter = email never arrives |
| The email address has no spaces | takeo_inoue_re@yahoo.com — no spaces anywhere |
| You can add a message before forwarding | Type your message above the forwarded email text |
| Forwarding does NOT delete the original email | The email stays in your inbox — do not worry |

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Mistake 1 — Typing the wrong email address**
- Always double-check: **takeo_inoue_re@yahoo.com**
- Common errors: forgetting the underscore `_`, missing the `@`, or misspelling `yahoo`

**Mistake 2 — Pressing "Reply" instead of "Forward"**
- **Reply** sends back to the person who sent you the email
- **Forward** sends to a NEW person (like Takeo)
- Make sure you tap/click **Forward**

**Mistake 3 — Forgetting to press Send**
- After typing the address, you must press **Send** or the email will not go

## You Did It!

After you press Send, the email goes to **takeo_inoue_re@yahoo.com** automatically.
You do not need to do anything else.

If you are not sure it worked, you can check your **Sent** folder:
- iPhone Mail: tap **Mailboxes** → tap **Sent**
- Gmail app: tap the three lines (☰) → tap **Sent**
- Gmail on computer: click **Sent** on the left side
- Outlook: click **Sent Items** on the left side

If the email is in your Sent folder, it was forwarded successfully!
