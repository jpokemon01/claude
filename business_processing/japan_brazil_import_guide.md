# Importing Japanese Goods to Brazil — Process Guide

Products covered: manga/comics, toys, children's clothing

---

## Critical Path (New Importer)

| Month | Task |
|---|---|
| 1–2 | Register company (CNPJ), apply for RADAR (importer ID with Receita Federal) |
| 2–4 | Submit toys/clothing samples to INMETRO-accredited lab for certification |
| 3–4 | Identify Japanese suppliers, request samples, engage freight forwarder |
| 4–6 | INMETRO certificates issued; prepare Portuguese label templates |
| 5–6 | Place first production order; file Licença de Importação (LI) in SISCOMEX |
| 6–7 | Goods ship from Japan (~35–45 days sea freight to Santos) |
| 7–8 | Customs clearance at Santos; pay duties; deliver to warehouse |
| 8+ | Goods available for sale |

**First shipment: ~5–8 months. Ongoing shipments: ~65–90 days.**

---

## Taxes by Category

### Manga / Comics
- **Total tax: ~0%** (constitutional book immunity — Lei Kandir / LC 87/1996)
- II: 0%, IPI: 0%, PIS: 0%, COFINS: 0%, ICMS: 0%
- Effective added cost: ~7–8% (logistics/broker fees only)

### Toys
- **Effective tax burden: ~55–65% of CIF value**
- II: 20%, PIS: 2.1%, COFINS: 9.65%, ICMS: ~18% (SP) — all stacked
- NCM codes: 9503.00.xx (toys), 9504.50.00 (games/puzzles)

### Children's Clothing
- **Effective tax burden: ~55–65% of CIF value**
- II: 35%, PIS: 2.1%, COFINS: 9.65%, ICMS: ~18% (SP)
- NCM codes: 6111.xx.xx (knit), 6209.xx.xx (woven)

---

## INMETRO Requirements

### Toys — Portaria INMETRO 563/2016
- Mandatory certification via OCP (accredited certification body) before sale
- Testing against ABNT NBR ISO 8124-1/2/3 (physical safety, flammability, chemical migration)
- Cost: BRL 8,000–25,000 per product model
- Timeline: 3–6 months per model
- Certificate validity: 1–3 years

### Children's Clothing — Portaria INMETRO 144/2015
- Self-Declaration of Conformity (DCC) for most categories
- Third-party lab testing required for nightwear
- Absolute prohibition: drawstrings/cords on hoods (ages 0–14)
- Cost: BRL 2,000–8,000

| Document | Source |
|---|---|
| Commercial Invoice | Japanese supplier |
| Packing List | Japanese supplier |
| Bill of Lading / Air Waybill | Shipping carrier |
| Certificate of Origin | Japanese Chamber of Commerce / JETRO |
| Export Declaration | Japan Customs (via NACCS) |
| Licença de Importação (LI) | Filed in SISCOMEX before shipment |
| Declaração de Importação (DI) / DUIMP | Filed by customs broker at arrival |
| INMETRO Certificate | INMETRO-accredited OCP |

## Labeling Requirements (Portuguese)

### All products
- Country of origin: "Fabricado no Japão"
- Brazilian importer name + CNPJ

### Toys (additional)
- Age recommendation and safety warnings in Portuguese
- INMETRO seal
- "Conservar fora do alcance de crianças menores de 3 anos" (if small parts)

### Children's Clothing (additional)
- Fiber composition in Portuguese (e.g. "100% Algodão")
- Care instructions per ABNT NBR 10,592
- Brazilian sizing per ABNT NBR 7,483 (not Japanese sizing)

## RADAR Registration (Importer ID)

Required before any import. Managed by Receita Federal.

| Type | Annual Limit |
|---|---|
| Limitado | USD 150,000/year |
| Ilimitado | No limit |

Process: file via Portal Siscomex → submit financials → 30–90 day approval

## Shipping Options

| Mode | Transit Time | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Sea freight (LCL) | 35–45 days | Regular shipments >2 CBM |
| Sea freight (FCL 20') | 35–45 days | Large volume (USD 2,500–5,000/container) |
| Air freight | 3–5 days | High-value/time-sensitive goods |
| Courier (DHL/FedEx) | 5–7 days | Test orders under USD 3,000 |

Main Brazil entry port: **Santos (SP)** for sea; **Guarulhos (GRU)** for air.

---

## Intellectual Property — Manga

- Importing **original Japanese-language editions** for collectors: generally permissible
- Importing **Portuguese editions** that compete with licensed Brazilian publishers (Panini, JBC, NewPOP): infringement risk
- **Absolutely prohibited:** sexual content involving minors (ECA — criminal liability)
- Counterfeits seized at customs under Lei 9.279/1996
