# eBay Checklist: Buying an Unlocked iPhone 15 for Metro PCS

---

## 1. Unlock Status — Most Critical

| What to Check | What to Look For |
|---|---|
| "Unlocked" in title/description | Must say **Factory Unlocked** — not "Carrier Unlocked" or "Network Unlocked" |
| Factory unlocked | Unlocked by Apple from the start — works on any carrier including Metro PCS |
| Carrier unlocked | Was locked then unlocked by carrier — sometimes has issues; avoid if possible |

Metro PCS uses **T-Mobile's network** — US models include all required T-Mobile/Metro PCS bands.

---

## 2. Model Number — Verify US Compatibility

| Model | Region | Metro PCS Compatible? |
|---|---|---|
| **A2846** | USA (Verizon) | Yes — best choice |
| **A3090** | USA (T-Mobile/Metro) | Yes — best choice |
| A2848 | International | May lack some US bands |
| A2849 | International | May lack some US bands |

Always verify the model number in the listing. US models (A2846, A3090) are safest for Metro PCS.

---

## 3. Carrier Lock / Blacklist Status

| Check | How |
|---|---|
| Carrier lock | Ask seller to confirm IMEI is unlocked before buying |
| Blacklisted / stolen | Run IMEI on **IMEI.info** or **Swappa IMEI checker** (free) |
| Lost/stolen report | Blacklisted phones show "Bad ESN/IMEI" — Metro PCS will reject activation |

**Request the IMEI from the seller before buying** — any legitimate seller will provide it.

---

## 4. iCloud / Activation Lock

| What | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| iCloud locked = previous owner's Apple ID still on phone | Cannot set up — the phone is unusable |
| How to verify | Ask seller for screenshot: Settings → General (no MDM), and iCloud signed out |
| Red flag | Listing says "sold as-is" or "for parts" — often means iCloud locked |

Never buy an iPhone that shows an Activation Lock screen — there is no legal way to remove it.

---

## 5. Seller Reputation

| Check | Standard |
|---|---|
| Feedback score | Minimum **98%+ positive** |
| Number of reviews | At least **50+ reviews** |
| Feedback content | Read recent negative reviews specifically |
| Return policy | Must have **30-day return policy** — avoid "no returns" |
| Seller location | US-based sellers preferred |

---

## 6. Phone Condition

| Grade | Description |
|---|---|
| New / Sealed | Never opened; highest price |
| Like New / Open Box | Opened but unused; good value |
| **Excellent / Grade A** | Minor scratches, fully functional — best value |
| Good / Grade B | Visible scratches, no cracks |
| Fair / Grade C | Noticeable wear — avoid unless budget is priority |

Request actual photos of: front screen, back glass, all corners, charging port.

---

## 7. Battery Health

| Level | Assessment |
|---|---|
| **90–100%** | Excellent |
| **85–89%** | Good — acceptable |
| **80–84%** | Degraded — factor in possible replacement |
| Below 80% | Apple considers degraded — battery replacement ~$99 |

Ask seller for screenshot: Settings → Battery → Battery Health. If they can't provide it — pass.

---

## 8. Storage Size

| Size | Best For |
|---|---|
| 128GB | Most users — sufficient for daily use |
| 256GB | Recommended if storing lots of photos/videos |
| 512GB | Heavy users / no cloud storage |

No microSD slot — storage cannot be expanded after purchase.

---

## 9. Payment Method

| Method | Protection |
|---|---|
| **eBay checkout / PayPal** | Full eBay Money Back Guarantee — use this |
| Credit card through eBay | Chargeback option as backup |
| Venmo / Zelle / wire transfer | No buyer protection — avoid completely |

---

## 10. Red Flags — Avoid These Listings

- Price significantly below market (iPhone 15 128GB ~$400–550 used)
- "For parts or not working"
- "Activation Lock" mentioned anywhere
- No return policy
- No model number in listing
- Seller asks to communicate outside eBay
- Stock photos instead of actual product photos

---

## Quick Pre-Purchase Checklist

- [ ] Title says "Factory Unlocked"
- [ ] Model number is A2846 or A3090 (US model)
- [ ] IMEI verified clean on IMEI.info
- [ ] No iCloud/Activation Lock confirmed
- [ ] Seller has 98%+ feedback, 50+ reviews
- [ ] 30-day return policy
- [ ] Battery health 85%+
- [ ] Actual product photos provided
- [ ] Paying through eBay checkout only

---

## After Purchase: Moving Metro PCS Plan

1. Insert your Metro PCS SIM into the new iPhone 15
2. If no physical SIM, go to Metro PCS app or store to transfer your number to eSIM
3. Go to Settings → Cellular → confirm carrier shows "Metro by T-Mobile"
4. If not activating automatically, call Metro PCS: **1-888-863-8768**
