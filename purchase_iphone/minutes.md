**User:** I would like to purchase unlocked iPhone 15 from eBay to move my Metro PCS plan. What is the checking point for eBay to select?

**Claude:** Provided comprehensive eBay buying checklist:
- **Unlock status:** Must be Factory Unlocked (not carrier unlocked)
- **Model number:** A2846 or A3090 (US models) — compatible with Metro PCS/T-Mobile network
- **IMEI check:** Verify clean (not blacklisted/stolen) on IMEI.info before buying; request IMEI from seller
- **iCloud/Activation Lock:** Confirm no Activation Lock — phone is unusable if locked to previous owner's Apple ID
- **Seller:** 98%+ feedback, 50+ reviews, 30-day return policy, US-based preferred
- **Battery health:** 85%+ minimum; ask for screenshot
- **Condition:** Grade A (Excellent) is best value sweet spot
- **Payment:** eBay checkout only — never Venmo/Zelle/wire transfer
- **Red flags:** Below-market price, "for parts," no return policy, stock photos, seller requests off-eBay contact
- **After purchase:** Insert Metro PCS SIM or transfer to eSIM via Metro PCS app/store

**User:** eBay Refurbished means that No iCloud/Activation Lock?

**Claude:** Explained the difference:
- **eBay Certified Refurbished:** Activation Lock guaranteed removed — eBay-vetted, 2-year warranty, safest option
- **eBay Refurbished (standard):** Not guaranteed by eBay — relies on seller integrity; reputable sellers always remove it but no formal backing
- **Recommendation:** Prioritize eBay Certified Refurbished for peace of mind; always verify IMEI on IMEI.info even for refurbished listings
